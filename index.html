Trainee Selection Process

In the trainee selection and enrolment process BASIS will go through following steps:

Step 1: Online Registration

Interested applicant can complete online registration using BASIS’s online platform. For online registration applicants will need to provide the following information:

> Personal details

> Educational Qualification

> All other related information of the trainee

Step 2: Short-listing through Written Test/ Interview:

Interested applicants will be invited to sit for admission test. The format of the admission test could be the face to face interview, written test or both. Applicants selected through the admission test will be notified for enrollment through SMS and email.

Step 3: Final Enrollment

> Selected applicants will be duly enrolled on submission of necessary documents including (i)Photocopy of NID (ii)Document relating to academic qualification (iii)Photo(Passport size);

> Their information will be uploaded on the SEIP Training Management System (TMS) by the training institutes. All the trainee selection and enrollment processes will be documented and preserved for monitoring as well as other administrative and financial purpose.

> A waiting list of suitable trainees will be maintained and considered in the upcoming/ next batches

In addition to regular activities, BASIS will arrange advocacy, social marketing, seminars, workshops as well as awareness campaign to raise awareness about the SEIP-BASIS training program.
